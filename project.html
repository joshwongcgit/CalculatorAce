About

History

The history section keeps track of your past calculations. This will enable users to have an easier and friendly way of solving and track long mathematical problems

Using history to reselect

You can click or tap on any elements to add to your current calculation. It allows you visualize previous results so you can input them into your next problem

Single and whole digits

Using or the hotkey Q, the numeric values can be reseelected in the history section as separate digits or as a whole.

Above: The single digit selection is enabled, allowing us to reselect previously used numbers in single digit form.

Above: The whole number selection is enabled, allowing us to select previously used whole numbers.
